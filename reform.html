Controls

Scale

Expand or shrink the selected notes' timings. Anchor sets the coordinate where notes expand from/shrink to.

Curve

Redistribute the selected notes' timings along one of the available curve types. Anchor controls whether this happens in Note-Range or Selection-Range.

Shift

Shift all of the selected notes forward or backward equally.

Overflow

Use available empty columns when necessary to avoid notes being overwritten.

Condense

Use as few columns as possible without notes being overwritten.

Redistribute

Redistribute note timings evenly. Anchor controls whether this happens in Note-Range or Selection-Range.

Collision Control

Determines which notes are preserved in the event of note collisions. The options are to keep earlier notes, later notes, notes which are selected, and notes which are not selected.

Anchor

Controls the coordinate from which notes are Scaled, and the range within which notes are Curved and Redistributed. (Note-Range: from the first to the last note in the selection. Selection-Range: from the top to the bottom of the selection box.)

Volume Transform

Remap Volume values to a new range and curve, and redistribute them evenly.

Panning Transform

Remap Panning values to a new range and curve, and redistribute them evenly.

FX Transform

Remap Local FX Amount values to a new range and curve, and redistribute them evenly.

Local Hotkeys

Playback the selected notes.

Playback from the edit cursor's position.
